About
updated March 2023

Hi, hello! My name is Hannah Yao, and I am a graduate of Computer Science and Applied Math at Stony Brook University. Currently I work at Nasdaq as a software engineer.

My favorite part about software/application development is how it is able to concretize concepts into something systematic and tangible -- In the way, being able to translate the intricate, subconcious process of human thinking into a verbalized, structured form of understanding is what I find beautiful.

I really like writing project documentation and find newer shiny tech stacks to be exciting and pleasant to the brain; however, I quite enjoy the mundane things too -- such as application maintenance, cleanup, or bug fixing (or what no one else wants to work on).

That aside, I spend time journaling, learning/reading depth psychology & poetry, and playing video games. I would describe myself as someone who is introspective, honest, and prone to being more wordy than neccessary.
