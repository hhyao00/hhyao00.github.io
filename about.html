About
updated 2020

Hi! My name is Hannah Yao, and I am a graduate student at Stony Brook University. My favorite aspect of computer science is how it is able to concretize abstract concepts (often taken for granted but implicitly present in everyday life) into something systematic and tangible. Sometimes I like to pretend I'm writing library or API code when I'm doing coursework.

Aside from computer science, I enjoy drawing and journaling. I also am very curious about depth psychology; specifically Carl Jung's psychological archtypes.
